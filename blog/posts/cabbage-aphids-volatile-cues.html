Behavioral Responses of Cabbage Aphids to Volatile Cues

January 15, 2024 Suleiman Mustapha

This research investigates how cabbage aphids respond to volatile cues emitted by plants under herbivory by conspecifics and heterospecifics. This study reveals fascinating insights into chemical communication in pest communities and provides crucial information for developing sustainable pest management strategies.
